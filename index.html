Welcome To Chumchunwatkhuanmeed
Chumchunwatkhuanmeed School is a long-established community school founded in 1946 and located in Village No. 2, Khuan Meet, Chana District, Songkhla. As an institution under the Khlong Phea Subdistrict Administration, this school is committed to providing accessible and high-quality education while supporting students’ academic growth and well-being. This school highlights its achievements through the Infographic of Innovations in School Management under the “One District, One Quality School” Policy for the Year 2025.

About Our School

School Identity & Philosophy

Philosophy

Good body, Good mind, Good deeds

Motto

"Study well, have discipline, possess good morals, be a leader in technology, follow the philosophy of sufficiency."

Identity

Creative thinking, bright mind, beautiful heart.

Uniqueness

Outstanding morals, living according to the Sufficiency Economy.

Chumchun Watkhuanmeed School promotes well-rounded growth through strong character, disciplined learning, and moral values. Guided by the Sufficiency Economy, the school encourages creative thinking and responsible use of technology. Its identity is represented by the green-and-white colors and the Bullet Wood tree, with the proud abbreviation S.K.M.
